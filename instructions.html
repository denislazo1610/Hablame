How to start

To use Hablame.com, you will need to create an account (Username and a password). Having an account will save your progress and your points. Then it is going to ask you to turn on your camera and microphone. This will make sure you can take fully advantage of the web site. Then it will ask you for your phone number. This will help us recover your account if you forget your username and password. Then, it will ask you the language that you speak. This will help us to know on what language you can teach. And finally, it will ask you what languages you want to learn
