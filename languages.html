Languages

English

English is the business language on the world. Knowing English can increase your chances of getting a good job, you can work for really well-known companies. English is the language of international communication. You can access to more internet with English

Chinese

Chinese is the most widely spoken language. It is also good for business. There 14% of the pupation of the world who speak that language. If you want to do a business in Asia, then this is your goal language!

German

German is the most widely spoken language in Europe. You can connect to 120 million native speakers, and there are many other people who are trying to learn that language. Fact: Germany has the 3rd strongest economy in the world and the 1st in exporting in the world.

Spanish

Countries that speak Spanish are traders, so it is good for business as well. You can access to the world of art and literature. You will be able to express yourself in many ways! Spanish will open to you a pool of romantic prospects!

Esperanto

This language was created 200 years ago! This is supposed to be the easiest language (simple grammar, easy pronunciation, phonetic writing system) to learn. It is a mix of many European languages. Esperanto will make other languages easier to learn! Esperanto does not belong to any country, but it is a neutral bridge between cultures!
